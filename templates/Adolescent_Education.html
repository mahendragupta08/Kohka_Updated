HEALTH CAMPS

India is home to 253 million adolescents, young people in the age group of 10 - 19 years who comprise 21% of the country's population.

We organize adolescent health education camps multiple times a year with reputed gynecologist Dr. Shilpa Bhiwapurkar, across villages and government schools in Seoni district, Madhya Pradesh. We also conduct eye checkup camps and basic health awareness drives.
