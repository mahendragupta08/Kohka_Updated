Our Story

WHO WE ARE

Kohka Foundation emerged out of a pursuit to inspire and support local tribal communities in India, to create a generational change.

Established in 2014, we are an unafraid to stand on the frontline in times of need to alleviate the citizens of India from crisis and need.

Contact Us

OUR VISION

To create generational change for rural underprivileged and marginalised communities.

OUR MISSION

To engage, empower and enrich.

OUR JOURNEY

From 2014, we have today covered 2 major cities (Mumbai and Nagpur) and multiple villages across Seoni District, MP.

"From The Founder's Desk"

Sanjay Nagar, Founder & Trustee

"Working in foreign banks for more than two decades, I quit my job to follow my passion of wildlife and photography. That brought me to Pench National Park, Madhya Pradesh."

"Seeing the poverty of the villagers, broken down government schools and lack of livelihood moved me and I knew I couldn't watch silently. Volunteering in the local government school in Kohka village soon turned into setting up of Kohka Foundation in the span of 3 years."

"In Kohka Foundation, my team and I bring to light the causes that we hold the closest to our hearts - secondary education, women's welfare and health, community development and rural employability.
With more than 8 years under our belt, I recognise that this is just the beginning of our work."
