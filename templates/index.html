Our Story

About Us:

Kohka Foundation is a Non-Profit Organisation working towards the generational upliftment of tribal and underprivileged sections of society in India.




Get Involved


With roots in Seoni, Madhya Pradesh which have now spread to Mumbai, Maharashtra - we have spent the last 8 years bringing our motto of "Engagement, Empowerment & Enrichment" to life. In the need of the hour, we have been focusing our relief efforts aimed at the Corona Virus pandemic since 2020.


Donate Now

Our Reach

Our major beneficiaries in Madhya Pradesh include tribal students, adolescents and women who do not possess employable skills and are financially non-independent.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, our main beneficiaries in Mumbai have been city-wide police departments, hospitals, transgender and sex worker communities, and visually impaired individuals.
